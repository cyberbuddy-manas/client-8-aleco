About Us

Aleco Doors and Windows was founded in 2014 with the goal of providing top-quality doors and windows to residential and commercial customers. The company's founders recognized the need for durable, energy-efficient, and stylish door and window options, and they set out to fill that need with their own brand "Aleco".

From the beginning, Aleco Doors and Windows focused on delivering outstanding customer service and a commitment to quality workmanship. This dedication to excellence paid off in the form of repeat customers and positive word-of-mouth referrals. Over the years, the company expanded its offerings to include a wider range of door and window options, as well as custom fabrication services for clients with unique needs.

One of the keys to Aleco Doors and Windows' success has been its commitment to using only the best materials available. Whether it's top-of-the-line hardware or the finest lumber and glass, the company's products are built to last and perform well for years to come. This focus on quality has earned the company a reputation for being a reliable and trustworthy provider of doors and windows.
